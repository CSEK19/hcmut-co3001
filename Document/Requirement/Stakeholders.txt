Stakeholders
  - End users: Restaurant customers, Restaurant staffs
  - System managers: IT staffs, Restaurant manager
  - System owners: restaurant owners
  - External stakeholders

IT staffs:		 Implement and maintain the system
Restaurant owner:	 Manage staffs, list of food, revenue
Restaurant staffs:	 Manage receipts, list of food, payment, booking and customers service
Restaurant customers:	 Order food, reserve table, give feedback
